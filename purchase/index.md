To purchase a high resolution digital copy of a photo click on the 'Add To Cart" button when viewing the larger version of a photo in one of the galleries. You can do this for one, or multiple photos, pricing varies by event/gallery.

Once you've selected the photo(s) you want to purchase, click the CART link in the top right corner of the page. You will be able to "check out" with PayPal.

Once we receive payment, please give us 2-3 days to get the high resolution photos (jpg files will be provided) together for you, you will receive an email from Chris with details on how to download the high res shot(s).

*Copyright:* Purchasing our photos will get you a non-watermarked photo for personal use. Commercial use options are available on a case by case basis.

If you have any trouble or questions, please feel free to reach out to me via my personal [contact page](https://chrishammond.com/Contact), we'll ask you details about event and individual so we can determine which photo(s) you want.
